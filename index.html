Over 72% of Sylhet district is under water with over 6 million people have been affected by severe flooding in Bangladesh.
According to the Sylhet Water Development Board (WDB), river water has never risen so much since 2008. This is the biggest flood in the last 3 decades and WDB officials fear the flood situation could worsen further if the rains continue upstream
